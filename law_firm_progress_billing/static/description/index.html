Law Firms Practice with Customer Progress Billing

Progress Billing to Customer for Advocate Cases and Hearings

Legal Practice | Lawyer Practice with Progress Bill to Customer

This app allows you to create progress billings and send to your customer for law firm / legal practice business.


Main Features:
  • System allows you to create progress billing from a job costing sheet where you can send progress billings/invoices to your customer.
  • For a more detailed process of progress billings from the job cost sheet you can check https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/14.0/odoo_job_costing_progress_billing/ screenshots and video.
  • For details about progress billing (without using job cost sheet) you can check https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/14.0/odoo_customer_progress_billing/screenshots and video.
  • For more details please see the video and check below screenshots.
