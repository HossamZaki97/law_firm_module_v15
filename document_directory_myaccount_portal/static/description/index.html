Share Document on Portal to Your Customers / Vendors / Partners

Main Features:

  • This module allows your customers/suppliers to download documents from My Account Portal of your website. Only documents shared with customers / suppliers / partners are allowed to view and download by them from the Portal My Account of your website.
  • You will have to set partners/contacts/customers/vendors on Share on Portal field on document/attachment in backend and then they can see it on the portal of your website and download it.
